Evan W. Olson, Creative

I believe we were created to be creative. Our ability to tell stories is one of the many gifts we have been given as humans. I therefore take this responsibilty seriously.

In all of my mediums, it is my goal to create moments of peace, joy, goodness, and compassion. I accomplish this by creating worlds we long for, or by revealing our shortcomings so that we can learn, lament, and build empathy and sympathy

Art is my life and I am happy to work with others to craft their visions. I strive for understanding throughout my life and believe that this skill helps me to help you.
